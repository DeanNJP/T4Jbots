Tech 4 Justice Clinic

This page explains what the clinic is and how people can access support.

What the clinic does

• Helps people clarify what happened
• Helps identify likely complaint pathways
• Helps turn notes into a clear complaint summary
• Explains what to expect after lodging

Who it’s for

People experiencing discrimination who want help understanding options and next steps.
