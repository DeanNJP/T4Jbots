Discrimination complaints

This page is a practical checklist. Keep it short. Make it easy to scan.

What to write down

• What happened (in order)
• Dates, times, and locations
• Who was involved (roles if you prefer)
• What was said/done that felt discriminatory
• What harm it caused (financial, emotional, safety, exclusion)
• What outcome you want (apology, change, compensation, work/school outcome)

Evidence that helps

• Emails / texts / messages
• Screenshots and photos
• Letters or decision notices
• Policies or guidelines you were given
• Medical evidence (if relevant)
• Witness notes (even informal)
• A simple timeline

Drafting tips

• Put the outcome you want near the top.
• Use short paragraphs and headings.
• Include key quotes (if you can).
• Stick to the facts. Avoid long backstory.
• Keep copies of everything you send.
