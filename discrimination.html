Discrimination complaints

This page is written to be edited over time. Keep it short, practical, and plain-English.

What to write down

• What happened (step-by-step)
• Dates, times, and locations
• Who was involved (roles if you prefer)
• What was said / done that felt discriminatory
• What harm it caused (financial, emotional, safety, exclusion)
• What outcome you want (apology, change, compensation, job outcome)

Evidence that helps

• Emails / messages / letters
• Screenshots, photos, recordings (if lawful)
• Policies and forms you were given
• Medical evidence (if relevant)
• Witness notes (even informal)
• A simple timeline

Drafting tips

• Be specific: quote key phrases if you can.
• Avoid long backstory. Focus on what happened + why it matters.
• State the outcome you want in one sentence near the top.
• Keep copies of everything you send.
